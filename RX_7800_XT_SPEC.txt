## AMD Radeon RX 7800 XT Specifications  
Architecture: RDNA 3.5  
Fabrication Process: 5 nm GCD + 6 nm MCD (Chiplet)  
Compute Units: 60  
Stream Processors: 3840  
Game Clock: 2.4 GHz  
Boost Clock: 2.5 GHz  

## Memory & Bandwidth  
VRAM: 16 GB GDDR6  
Memory Speed: 20 Gbps  
Memory Bus Width: 256-bit  
Memory Bandwidth: ~640 GB/s (with Infinity Cache)  
Infinity Cache: 80 MB  

## Power & Thermals  
TDP / TBP: 240 W  
Typical Power Draw: ~220-240 W  
Recommended PSU: 650 W  
Power Connectors: 1 × 8-pin  
Max Operating Temperature: 95 °C  

## Performance Metrics  
FP32 Compute: ~33 TFLOPS  
Target Workloads: 1440p Ultra, 4K High  
Ray Tracing: Improved RDNA 3.5 vs prior generation  

## Display & IO  
Outputs: 3 × DisplayPort 2.1, 1 × HDMI 2.1a  
PCIe Interface: PCIe 4.0 x16  
AV1 Decode: Yes  
VR Support: Yes  

## Feature Support  
API: DirectX 12 Ultimate, Vulkan 1.3, OpenCL 3.0  
Technologies: FSR 3, Smart Access Memory, Resizable BAR, Radeon Boost  

## Physical & Build  
Form Factor: 2-slot design, ~260 mm length (varies by board partner)  
Cooling: Dual/triple‐fan (depending on board partner)  

## Launch Info  
Release Date: August 2023  
Launch Price: US$549 (Suggested MSRP)  
Market Target: Upper‐Mainstream / Entry High‐End 4K Gaming
