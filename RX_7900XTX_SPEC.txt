## AMD Radeon RX 7900 XTX Specifications
Architecture: RDNA 3
Fabrication Process: 5 nm GCD + 6 nm MCD (Chiplet Design)
Compute Units: 96
Game Clock: 2.3 GHz
Boost Clock: 2.5 GHz
Stream Processors: 6144
Ray Accelerators: 96 (2nd-Gen)
AI Accelerators: 192

## Memory & Bandwidth
VRAM: 24 GB GDDR6
Memory Speed: 20 Gbps
Memory Bus Width: 384-bit
Memory Bandwidth: 960 GB/s (with Infinity Cache)
Infinity Cache: 96 MB

## Power & Thermals
TDP / TBP: 355 W
Typical Power Draw: 320–355 W
Recommended PSU: 850 W
Power Connectors: 2 × 8-pin
Maximum Operating Temperature: 90 °C

## Performance Metrics
FP32 Compute: 61.4 TFLOPS
Ray Tracing Throughput: Improved vs RDNA2
Target Workloads: 4K Ultra Gaming, Creator Work, AI Acceleration

## Display & IO
Outputs: 3 × DisplayPort 2.1, 1 × HDMI 2.1
PCIe Interface: PCIe 4.0 x16
AV1: Decode & Encode Enabled
VR Support: Yes

## Feature Support
API: DirectX 12 Ultimate, Vulkan 1.3, OpenGL 4.6
Technologies: FSR 3 Frame Generation, Smart Access Memory, Fidelity AI

## Physical & Build
Form Factor: 3-slot, ~287–305 mm (varies by vendor)
Cooling: Triple-fan vapor chamber designs

## Launch Info
Release Date: December 13, 2022
Launch Price: US$999
Market Target: High-end 4K Gaming Value Alternative to RTX 4090
